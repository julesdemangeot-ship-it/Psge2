\begin{abstract}
We present a complete covariant extension of General Relativity where spacetime possesses a macroscopic elastic resistance, modeled by a scalar field with derivative couplings. The action includes a rigidity term $\alpha(\partial\psi)^4/M_{\rm Pl}^4$ and a non-minimal coupling $\xi R\psi^2$. The model resolves $H_0$ and $\sigma_8$ tensions, matches DESI, and makes testable predictions for LISA and EHT.
\end{abstract}
\end{@twocolumnfalse}
